Ingredients:
 - organic carrots
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 tblspoons mayonnaise or yoghurt
 - 1 clove garlic
 - pepper 

Directions:
 1. Skin and wash carrots and grate.

 2. Add salt and leave to sit for 20 minutes.

 3. Crush garlic and mix with mayonnaise or yoghurt

 4. Mix everything, add pepper to taste.
